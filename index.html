About us

Raging Death Films is small low-budget film production company based in the Isle of Man.

Starting out in 2009 we begin by making the short film "Get Dead" for the 2 Days Later short horror film competition, which inspired us to continue writing and explore the possibilties of making short films on a miniscule budget.

In 2010 we entered the competition again with the short ‘In Mourning’. Both of these films have helped us develop has film makers.

Since then we have been on hiatus. Hopefully one day we will turn.

Until then you can enjoy our films on this website.
